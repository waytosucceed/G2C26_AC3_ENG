Silent Letters

Silent letters are letters in words that we write but don’t say out loud. They help us spell words correctly, even though we don’t hear them when we speak.
Here are some important rules about “Silent Letters”:

👉 Silent “E”:
Rule: When a word has one vowel followed by one consonant and then an 'E', the 'E' is usually silent and makes the vowel say its name!
